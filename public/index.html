"The Mountains Are Calling, I Must Go."

John Muir’s immortal words inspire us in our exploration and creative endeavors in the expansive landscapes surrounding us. From cactus to clouds; and from palms to pines - we’re excited to share the magic of California’s Route 74 with you.

Kari Hay

Hair-Stylist & Dream Manifestor

Twenty years ago Kari followed her dream and chased the Sun to Southern California; with a passion for creating she brings a personal touch to hair design and self-care, and delights in offering creative opportunities for people to gather and explore.

Dean Hay

Event Planner & Animal-Lover Extraordinaire

With two decades in professional event-planning, and experience as one of Sea World's first animal-trainers; Dean brings a whale-rounded perspective along with A+ dad-jokes in helping to plan your adventures throughout his mountain-desert home.
